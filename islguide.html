📘 Islamic Studies Mega Revision Guide

1. Shariah & Maqasid al-Shariah

  • Literal Meaning: Clear path to follow.
  • Technical Meaning: Complete system governing Muslim life.
  • Main Divisions: Ibadat + Muamalat.
  • Core Moral Principle: Amr bil-Ma'ruf & Nahi anil-Munkar.
MEMORISE → “Religion, Life, Intellect, Lineage, Wealth”
Maqasid Meaning
Hifz al-Din Protection of Religion
Hifz al-Nafs Protection of Life
Hifz al-Aql Protection of Intellect
Hifz al-Nasl Protection of Lineage
Hifz al-Mal Protection of Wealth

2. Sources of Islamic Law

Category Sources Basis
Primary Quran + Sunnah Divine Revelation
Secondary Ijma + Qiyas Human Reasoning
  • Ijma: Consensus of scholars.
  • Qiyas: Analogy using shared Illah.
  • Ijtihad: Independent reasoning for new issues.
Qiyas Formula → Asl → Far’ → Illah → Hukm

3. Quran & Sunnah

  • Quran revealed over 23 years.
  • 114 Surahs.
  • Makki Surahs = Belief & Tawheed.
  • Madani Surahs = Laws & Society.
Sunnah Type Meaning
Sunnah Mu’akkadah Strongly emphasized
Ghair Mu’akkadah Less emphasized
Mandoob Recommended acts

4. Jihad vs Qital

Jihad Qital
From Jahada = struggle From Qatala = fighting
Broad concept Specific armed combat
Spiritual + Social + Moral Purely physical warfare
Usually Fard al-Ayn Usually Fard al-Kifayah
Greater Jihad = Against Self Lesser Jihad = Armed Defense

4 Principles

  • Only State can declare Jihad.
  • Purpose must be ending oppression.
  • Treaties must be honored.
  • Resources & success prospects required.

5. Governance & Human Rights

  • Tawhid: Sovereignty belongs to Allah.
  • Karama: Human dignity for all.
  • Shura: Consultation.
  • Adl: Justice.
  • Maslaha: Public welfare.
  • Amana: Leadership is a trust.
ISLAMIC GOVERNANCE = Shura + Adl + Maslaha + Amana

7. Peacebuilding & Non-Violence

Concept Meaning
Negative Peace Absence of violence only
Positive Peace Justice + Equality + Harmony
Direct Violence Visible harm
Indirect Violence Structural oppression
  • Honor killings = Direct violence.
  • Feudal oppression = Structural violence.
  • Ghost schools = Structural violence.
  • Islam condemns Zulm & Baghy.
Positive Peace = Adl + Ihsan

🔥 TOUGH MCQs SECTION 🔥

1. Which concept MOST directly establishes that no human may claim ultimate sovereignty?

A) Adl
B) Tawhid
C) Shura
D) Maslaha

Answer: B) Tawhid

2. Which battle primarily demonstrated cross-cultural innovation?

A) Badr
B) Uhud
C) Khandaq
D) Hunayn

Answer: C) Khandaq

3. The MOST accurate description of Negative Peace is:

A) Justice and equality
B) End of oppression
C) Temporary absence of violence
D) Economic prosperity

Answer: C) Temporary absence of violence

4. Which Maqasid directly relates to protection of reasoning ability?

A) Hifz al-Nasl
B) Hifz al-Mal
C) Hifz al-Aql
D) Hifz al-Din

Answer: C) Hifz al-Aql

5. Which event proved that apparent compromise may become strategic victory?

A) Badr
B) Hijrah
C) Treaty of Hudaybiyyah
D) Uhud

Answer: C) Treaty of Hudaybiyyah

6. The Quranic principle forbidding wastefulness is:

A) Zulm
B) Israf
C) Amanah
D) Mizan

Answer: B) Israf

7. Which source of Islamic law depends primarily on rational justification?

A) Quran
B) Sunnah
C) Ijma
D) Wahy

Answer: C) Ijma

8. Which principle makes leadership a sacred responsibility?

A) Karama
B) Amana
C) Shura
D) Qital

Answer: B) Amana

9. Which phase of the Prophet’s mission focused mainly on Aqeedah and Tazkiyah?

A) Medinan Phase
B) Abbasid Era
C) Meccan Phase
D) Khilafah Period

Answer: C) Meccan Phase

10. Which one is considered INDIRECT violence?

A) Terrorism
B) Acid attack
C) Honor killing
D) Denial of girls' education

Answer: D) Denial of girls' education
